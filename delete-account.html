Account & Data Deletion

At PhishGuard, we value your privacy and give you full control over your data. You can delete your account and all associated data at any time.

How to delete your data

Option 1: In the App (Recommended)

1. Open the PhishGuard app.
2. Tap your profile icon (top right) on the Home Screen.
3. Select Delete Account.
4. Confirm your password if prompted.

Option 2: Email Request

If you no longer have access to the app, you can request data deletion via email. We will process your request within 7 business days.

Request Deletion via Email

What data is deleted?

Upon deletion, the following data is permanently removed from our servers:

  • Your account profile (Name and Email)
  • Your quiz progress, scores, and levels
  • Your achievement history and streaks
  • Your leaderboard entries

Note: This action is permanent and cannot be undone.
